Welcome To Branham Law Offices!

Ever since Branham Law, PLLC, was established in 2001, we have been dedicated to ensuring our clients are treated fairly under the law. When you visit our office in Mesa, we work closely with you to help you meet your legal goals and put you at ease. We focus our practice on where we feel we can help most, including areas such as:

Family Law

Compassionate guidance and strong advocacy for all family law issues.
Criminal Law

We handle criminal defense cases, ensuring the best outcome for our clients.
Personal Injury

Assisting with injury claims to secure the compensation you deserve.
Civil Litigation

Expert representation for resolving disputes in and out of court.
Immigration Law

Navigating complex immigration processes to help you move forward.
Real Estate Law

Comprehensive legal assistance for all property-related transactions.

Attorney Marlon Branham is an extremely knowledgeable trial lawyer with an excellent record of success trying and settling cases in Arizona. Our clients receive the high-quality service associated with a large law firm — without having to navigate the confusing bureaucracy many of these firms have in place. Prior to going into law, Mr. Branham spent time working for a large insurance company, seeing firsthand how unfair the market is for the average working citizen. No matter what your case involves, he works hard to even the playing field and provide the quality legal representation every person deserves.
